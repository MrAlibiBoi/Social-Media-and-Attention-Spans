Scrolled Away:
Social Media & the
Shrinking Attention Span

Created by Calen Nowroozi  ·  University of South Florida  ·  2025

We as a society, are in the age of life permanently with social media. As younger generations evolve, we desire the use of social media during prime times of boredom, awkwardness and conflicts in order to distract ourselves from whatever is happening in front of us. This has shown to come with the cost of addiction to our devices causing many of this generation to have bad short-term attention spans. This website has been made to discuss and present my research of the harms that social media can have on our attention span, but also show how we can improve our attention spans and I finally give my personal thoughts in the end.
